# Email

In the **Email** module you manage your email communication directly in Nuxbe.

## Overview

Navigate to **Mail** via the sidebar. You will see an overview of your emails.

![Email inbox](../screenshots/59-email-inbox.png)

## Sidebar

The left sidebar contains:

- **Search** - Search for email folders or accounts
- **Folders** - Your email folders (e.g. All Messages, Inbox, Sent, Drafts)
- **Fetch New Messages** - Click to manually check for new emails

## Message List

The main area shows your emails in a table with the following columns:

- **Date** - Date the email was received or sent
- **From** - Sender of the email
- **To** - Recipient of the email
- **Subject** - Email subject line
- **Text** - Preview of the email content
- **Communication Type** - Type of communication

## Search Emails

Use the search field above the table to search emails by subject, sender, recipient or content.

## Read an Email

1. Click on an email in the list to open it.
2. The full email content is displayed.

## Compose a New Email

1. Click **Fetch New Messages** or use the compose function.
2. Enter the recipient, subject and message body.
3. Click **Send**.
