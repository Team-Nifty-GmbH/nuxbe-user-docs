# Manage Leads

The lead list shows all sales contacts and their current status in the sales pipeline.

## Open the Lead List

1. Click **Sales** in the sidebar, then **Leads**.

   ![Leads overview with sales contacts](../screenshots/11-leads-list.png)

2. The table shows all leads with the following columns:
   - **Name** - Lead name
   - **Status** - Current stage in the sales process
   - **Contact** - Linked contact
   - **Created At** - Creation date

## Search and Filter Leads

- Use the search field above the table to find leads quickly.
- Use the filter row below the column headers to filter by individual columns.

## Create a New Lead

1. Click **New** in the top right corner.
2. Fill in the fields (name, contact, status, etc.).
3. Click **Save**.
