# Contacts

In the **Contacts** module you manage your customers, suppliers and their addresses.

## Overview

Navigate to **Contacts** via the sidebar. You will see a tabular overview of all contacts with columns such as customer number, name, debtor number, payment type and more.

![Contact list with all contacts](../screenshots/06-contacts-list.png)

## Features

- **Search** - Use the search field above the table to find contacts by name, customer number or other criteria.
- **Saved Filters** - Click **Saved Filters** to save and reuse frequently used filter settings.
- **New Contact** - Click **New** to create a new contact.

## Contact Details

Click on a contact in the list to open the detail view.

![Detail view of a contact](../screenshots/07-contact-detail.png)

In the detail view you will find:

- **Master Data** - Name, customer number, debtor number and other basic data
- **Addresses** - All stored addresses of the contact
- **Communication** - Email addresses, phone numbers and other contact options
- **Orders** - Linked orders and invoices
- **Bank Details** - Stored bank information
