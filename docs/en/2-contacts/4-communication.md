# Communication

In the **Communication** tab you manage all contact methods such as email addresses, phone numbers and websites.

## View Communication

1. Open the [detail view](2-contact-detail.md) of a contact.
2. Switch to the **Communication** tab.

   ![Communication list of a contact](../screenshots/22-communication-list.png)

3. The table shows all stored contact methods.

## Add a New Entry

1. Click **New** in the communication tab.
2. Select the type (e.g. email, phone, mobile, website).
3. Enter the value.
4. Click **Save**.

## Edit an Entry

1. Click **Edit** next to the desired entry.
2. Change the value or type.
3. Click **Save**.
