# Serial Numbers

In the **Serial Numbers** section you manage serial numbers for your products.

## Overview

1. Navigate to **Products > Serial Numbers**.

   ![Serial numbers list](../screenshots/32-serial-numbers-list.png)

2. The table shows all recorded serial numbers with their product assignment.

## Search Serial Numbers

Use the search field to find serial numbers quickly.
