# Calendar

In the **Calendar** module you manage appointments and keep track of upcoming events.

## Overview

Navigate to **Calendars** via the sidebar. You will see a calendar view with all appointments.

![Calendar with month view](../screenshots/58-calendar.png)

## Calendar Sidebar

On the left side you find:

- **Create Calendar** - Create a new calendar
- **My Calendars** - Your personal calendars
- **Other** - Shared or team calendars

Toggle calendars on or off to show or hide their appointments in the view.

## Views

Switch between views using the buttons at the top of the calendar:

- **Day** - Detailed daily view with hourly time slots
- **Week** - Weekly overview with all appointments per day
- **Month** - Monthly overview showing all appointments at a glance

## Navigate Between Dates

- Use the **arrow buttons** to move forward or backward in time.
- Click **Today** to return to the current date.

## Create an Appointment

1. Click on an empty time slot in the calendar.
2. Enter the appointment details (title, time, participants).
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit an Appointment

1. Click on an existing appointment in the calendar.
2. Change the desired fields.
3. Click **Save**.
