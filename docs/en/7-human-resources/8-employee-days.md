# Employee Days

The employee days overview shows daily working hour summaries for all employees.

## Open the Overview

1. Navigate to **Human Resources > Employee Days**.

   ![Employee days list](../screenshots/64-employee-days-list.png)

## Columns

The table shows the following columns:

- **Employee > Name** - Employee name
- **Date** - The working day
- **Target Hours** - Contractually agreed hours for the day
- **Actual Hours** - Actually worked hours
- **Used Holiday Days** - Holiday days used on this date
- **Used Sick Days** - Sick days recorded on this date
- **Plus/Minus Overtime** - Difference between target and actual hours
- **Plus/Minus Absence Hours** - Difference in absence hours
- **Actions** - Click **View** to open the detailed day view

## Search and Filter

- Use the search field to find entries by employee name.
- Use the filter row below the column headers to filter by date, employee or other criteria.
