# Absence Requests

In the **Absence Requests** section you manage holiday and absence requests.

## Overview

1. Navigate to **Human Resources > Absence Requests**.

   ![Absence requests list](../screenshots/38-absence-requests-list.png)

2. The table shows all absence requests with their status (requested, approved, rejected).

## Submit a New Request

1. Click **New**.
2. Select the **Absence Type** (e.g. holiday, sick leave, special leave).
3. Enter the period (**From** and **To**).
4. Click **Save**.

## Approve or Reject a Request

1. Open the request by clicking on the entry.
2. Review the details.
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
