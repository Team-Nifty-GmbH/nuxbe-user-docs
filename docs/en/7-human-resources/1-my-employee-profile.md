# My Employee Profile

In the **My Employee Profile** section you view and edit your own employee profile.

## Open Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Human Resources > My Employee Profile**.

   ![My employee profile](../screenshots/33-my-employee-profile.png)

2. You can see your personal data, working hours and absences.

## Edit Your Profile

1. Click **Edit** to adjust your data.
2. Change the desired fields.
3. Click **Save**.
