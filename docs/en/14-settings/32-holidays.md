# Holidays

Manage public holidays that are taken into account for working time calculations and absence planning.

## Open Holidays

1. Navigate to **Settings > Human Resources > Holidays**.

   ![Holidays list](../screenshots/83-settings-holidays.png)

## Create a Holiday

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter the name and date.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing holiday.
- Click **Delete** to remove a holiday.
