# Ledger Accounts

Manage the chart of accounts used throughout the accounting module.

## Open Ledger Accounts

1. Navigate to **Settings > Accounting > Ledger Accounts**.

   ![Ledger accounts list](../screenshots/88-settings-ledger-accounts.png)

2. The table shows all accounts with the following columns:
   - **Name** - Account name
   - **Number** - Account number
   - **Ledger Account Type** - Type (e.g. Revenue, Expense)
   - **Is Automatic** - Whether the account is assigned automatically

## Create a Ledger Account

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a name, number and select the account type.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing account.
- Click **Delete** to remove an account.
