# Bank Connections

Manage bank accounts used for transactions, direct debits and transfers.

## Open Bank Connections

1. Navigate to **Settings > Accounting > Bank Connections**.

   ![Bank connections list](../screenshots/73-settings-bank-connections.png)

2. The table shows all bank connections with the following columns:
   - **Is Active** - Whether the connection is enabled
   - **Name** - Account name
   - **IBAN** - International bank account number
   - **Account Holder** - Name of the account holder
   - **Bank Name** - Name of the bank
   - **BIC** - Bank identifier code
   - **ID** - Internal identifier
   - **Is Virtual** - Whether this is a virtual account

## Create a Bank Connection

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter the account details (name, IBAN, BIC, bank name).
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing connection.
- Click **Delete** to remove a connection.
