# Ticket Settings

Configure general ticket behaviour such as the auto-reply email template.

## Open Ticket Settings

1. Navigate to **Settings > Tickets > Ticket Settings**.

   ![Ticket settings](../screenshots/113-settings-ticket-settings.png)

2. The form contains:
   - **Auto Reply Email Template** - Select the template sent automatically when a new ticket is received

## Save Changes

1. Choose a template from the dropdown.
2. Click **Save**.
