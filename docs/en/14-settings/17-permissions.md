# Permissions

Manage roles and their associated permissions to control what users can access.

## Open Permissions

1. Navigate to **Settings > Users & Permissions > Permissions**.

   ![Permissions list](../screenshots/96-settings-permissions.png)

2. The table shows all roles with the following columns:
   - **Name** - Role name
   - **Guard Name** - Authentication guard (e.g. web, token, address)

## Create a Role

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a role name.
3. Click **Save**.

## Manage a Role

- **Assign Users** - Click **Assign Users** to add or remove users from the role.
- **Edit Permissions** - Click **Edit Permissions** to configure which actions the role allows.
- **Delete** - Click **Delete** to remove a custom role.
