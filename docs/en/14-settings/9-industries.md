# Industries

Manage the list of industries that can be assigned to contacts.

## Open Industries

1. Navigate to **Settings > General > Industries**.

   ![Industries list](../screenshots/84-settings-industries.png)

2. The table shows all industries with the **Name** column.

## Create an Industry

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter the industry name.
3. Click **Save**.
