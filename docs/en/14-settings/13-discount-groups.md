# Discount Groups

Manage discount groups that can be assigned to contacts or products to apply automatic discounts.

## Open Discount Groups

1. Navigate to **Settings > Orders > Discount Groups**.

   ![Discount groups list](../screenshots/79-settings-discount-groups.png)

2. The table shows all discount groups with the following columns:
   - **Name** - Group name
   - **Is Active** - Whether the group is enabled

## Create a Discount Group

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a name and configure the discount rules.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing discount group.
- Click **Delete** to remove a discount group.
