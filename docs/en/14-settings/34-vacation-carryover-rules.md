# Vacation Carryover Rules

Define rules for carrying over unused vacation days into the next year.

## Open Vacation Carryover Rules

1. Navigate to **Settings > Human Resources > Vacation Carryover Rules**.

   ![Vacation carryover rules list](../screenshots/119-settings-vacation-carryover-rules.png)

## Create a Rule

1. Click **New**.
2. Configure the maximum carryover days and expiry.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing rule.
- Click **Delete** to remove a rule.
