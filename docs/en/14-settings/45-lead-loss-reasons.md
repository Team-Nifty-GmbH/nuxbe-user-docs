# Lead Loss Reasons

Manage the reasons why a lead was lost, used for reporting and analysis.

## Open Lead Loss Reasons

1. Navigate to **Settings > Sales > Lead Loss Reasons**.

   ![Lead loss reasons list](../screenshots/86-settings-lead-loss-reasons.png)

## Create a Loss Reason

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a reason.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing reason.
- Click **Delete** to remove a reason.
