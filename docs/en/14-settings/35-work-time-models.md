# Work Time Models

Manage work time models that define expected working hours per day and week for employees.

## Open Work Time Models

1. Navigate to **Settings > Human Resources > Work Time Models**.

   ![Work time models list](../screenshots/122-settings-work-time-models.png)

## Create a Work Time Model

1. Click **New**.
2. Configure the daily and weekly working hours.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing model.
- Click **Delete** to remove a model.
