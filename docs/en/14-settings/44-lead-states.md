# Lead States

Manage the pipeline states a lead can go through, from initial contact to closing.

## Open Lead States

1. Navigate to **Settings > Sales > Lead States**.

   ![Lead states list](../screenshots/87-settings-lead-states.png)

## Create a State

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a name and set the sort order.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing state.
- Click **Delete** to remove a state.
