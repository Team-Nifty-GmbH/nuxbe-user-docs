# Warehouses

Manage warehouses used for stock management.

## Open Warehouses

1. Navigate to **Settings > Products > Warehouses**.

   ![Warehouses list](../screenshots/121-settings-warehouses.png)

## Create a Warehouse

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter a name and address details.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to modify an existing warehouse.
- Click **Delete** to remove a warehouse.
