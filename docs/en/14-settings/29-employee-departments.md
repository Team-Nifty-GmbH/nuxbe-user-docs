# Employee Departments

Manage the departments employees can be assigned to.

## Open Employee Departments

1. Navigate to **Settings > Human Resources > Employee Departments**.

   ![Employee departments list](../screenshots/81-settings-employee-departments.png)

## Create a Department

1. Click **New**.
2. Enter the department name.
3. Click **Save**.

## Edit or Delete

- Click **Edit** to rename a department.
- Click **Delete** to remove a department.
