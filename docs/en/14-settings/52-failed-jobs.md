# Failed Jobs

View and retry background jobs that have failed during processing.

## Open Failed Jobs

1. Navigate to **Settings > System > Failed Jobs**.

   ![Failed jobs list](../screenshots/82-settings-failed-jobs.png)

2. The table shows all failed jobs with their error message, queue and failure time.

## Retry a Job

- Click **Retry** to re-queue a failed job for another attempt.
