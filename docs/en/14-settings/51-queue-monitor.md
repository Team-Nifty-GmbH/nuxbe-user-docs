# Queue Monitor

Monitor the status of queued background jobs.

## Open Queue Monitor

1. Navigate to **Settings > System > Queue Monitor**.

   ![Queue monitor](../screenshots/103-settings-queue-monitor.png)

2. The table shows all queued jobs with their status, attempts and timestamps.
