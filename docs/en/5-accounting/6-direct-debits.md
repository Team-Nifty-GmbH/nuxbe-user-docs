# Direct Debits

In the **Direct Debits** section you manage direct debit collections for open invoices.

## Overview

1. Navigate to **Accounting > Direct Debit**.

   ![Direct debits list](../screenshots/29-direct-debits-list.png)

2. The table shows all planned and completed direct debit collections.

## Create a Direct Debit

1. Click **New**.
2. Select the orders for which a direct debit should be collected.
3. Review the amounts.
4. Click **Save**.
