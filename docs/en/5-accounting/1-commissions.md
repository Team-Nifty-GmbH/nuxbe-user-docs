# Commissions

In the **Commissions** section you manage commission settlements for sales representatives.

## Overview

1. Navigate to **Accounting > Commissions**.

   ![Commissions list](../screenshots/25-commissions-list.png)

2. The table shows all commissions with columns for employee, order, amount and status.
