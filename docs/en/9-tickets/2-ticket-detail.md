# Ticket Details

The detail view shows all information for a single ticket and allows you to edit it.

## Open the Detail View

1. Navigate to the [ticket list](1-manage-tickets.md).
2. Click on the desired ticket.

   ![Ticket details](../screenshots/44-ticket-detail.png)

## Sections

- **Title** - Subject of the ticket
- **Description** - Content of the request (rich text editor)
- **Ticket Type** - Type of ticket
- **Status** - Current processing status
- **Assigned To** - Assigned handlers

### Comments

The comments section is used for communication with the customer or internal team:

- **Public Comments** - Visible to the customer
- **Internal Comments** - Only visible to the team

### Working Hours

Recorded working hours for this ticket.

## Change Ticket Status

1. Select the new status from the dropdown.
2. Click **Save**.
